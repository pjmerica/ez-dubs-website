Arb Calculator

Plug in the YES prices on two markets covering the same outcome, set a bankroll, and get the optimal split that locks in equal payoffs on either resolution.

Inputs

How it works. A two-sided arb means buying YES on one market and NO on the other. "Buy NO at price p" is the same as paying 1 − p for a contract that returns $1 if the underlying resolves NO. The calculator picks the orientation that puts you on the cheaper side of each event and splits your bankroll so both legs return the same payoff. If price A + (1 − price B) ≥ 1 in both orientations, there's no arb.

Caveats not included. Fees (Kalshi/Polymarket ~3%, PredictIt ~12%) and the order book — the YES price you see is the mid, not the executable ask. A tight arb on paper may not survive either of those.
